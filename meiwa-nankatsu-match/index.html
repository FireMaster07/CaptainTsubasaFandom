SUMMARY

Meiwa faced off against Nankatsu in the opening match of the Elementary School
National Championship in Captain Tsubasa, marking Nankatsu's debut in Group 1.
With Genzo Wakabayashi sidelined due to injury, Yuzo Morisaki stepped in as
Nankatsu's goalkeeper.

Both team leaders, Tsubasa Ozora and Kojiro Hyuga netted five goals each. However,
It was the young talent Takeshi Sawada who clinched the victory for Meiwa by
scoring the decisive goal in the final moments of the match.-- his second of the game.
Taro Misaki also contributed with a goal and provided two assists to Tsubasa.
