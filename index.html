Kimi ni Sasageru Lullaby



"Kimi ni Sasageru Lullaby" (君に捧げるララバイ)
is an insert song for the 1983 Captain Tsubasa TV Series, which
is both a team theme in the Kids' Dream arc and Hyuga's Tiger Shot
theme in the Boys' Fight arc.
